Lasagna

Ingredients

  • 1 big Onion
  • 1-2 Carrots
  • 1 stem of Celery
  • One can of sieved Tomatos
  • One can of chunky Tomatos
  • Lasagna noodle sheets (you could of course make them by yourself)
  • 700 grams of minced beef
  • 200ml of red wine
  • 1-2 Tablespoons of tomato paste
  • Italian herbs
  • Salt and pepper
  • Sugar
  • Olive oil
  • Milk
  • Flower
  • Butter
  • Nutmeg

Now the fun Part

At first you have to finely dice the carrots, onion and celery into little cubes. Fry slowly in about four tablespoons of olive oil until soft.

Now add the mnced meat and chop it with a cooking spoon so it doesen't stick together into big balls. Fry and stirr it until fully cooked - you can fry it a bit further to get some roasted flavour.

When done add the tomato paste and let it get hot for a few seconds.

Add the red wine and let it simmer until almost fully reduced.

Its time for both of the tomato cans. Add all the herbs and spices (I recommend Italian) and a little bit of sugar - of course also salt and pepper - let it simmer for about 20 minutes. Don't forget to stirr from time to time. If it becomes ab bit to dry and thick, just add some red wine or water to keep it liquid. Also don't keep it to fluid because you want a stable Lasagna afterwards

While it simmers, you can parallel make the bechamel sauce
